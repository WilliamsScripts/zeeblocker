# 🔒 ZeeBlocker Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: November 16, 2025  
**Effective Date**: November 16, 2025

## Introduction

ZeeBlocker ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the ZeeBlocker Chrome extension.

## Information Collection

### What We Collect

ZeeBlocker stores data locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs:

#### Locally Stored Data
- **Settings**: Your preferences (dark mode, idle time threshold, etc.)
- **Tasks**: Task titles, descriptions, and time slots you create
- **Blocklist**: Websites you choose to block
- **Statistics**: Count of blocked sites and focus time (stored locally)
- **Access Logs**: Timestamps of blocked site access attempts (Child Safety mode only)

#### Data NOT Collected
- ❌ Browsing history beyond blocked site attempts
- ❌ Personal identifying information
- ❌ Passwords or sensitive credentials
- ❌ Content of web pages you visit
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device information
- ❌ Analytics or tracking data

### How We Collect It

Data is collected only through:
1. **Direct Input**: Information you enter in settings and tasks
2. **Extension Activity**: Sites blocked by Focus or Safety modes
3. **Chrome Sync**: Settings synced across your Chrome devices (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Site Blocking**: Check visited sites against your blocklist
- **Task Management**: Display and manage your tasks
- **Statistics**: Show productivity metrics
- **Notifications**: Alert you or configured recipients about blocked sites

### We Do NOT
- ❌ Sell your data
- ❌ Share data with third parties (except as configured by you)
- ❌ Use data for advertising
- ❌ Track you across websites
- ❌ Create user profiles for marketing

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- **Chrome Sync Storage**: Settings, tasks, blocklists (encrypted by Chrome, synced across your devices)
- **Chrome Local Storage**: Statistics and logs (local to each device)
- **No External Servers**: No data is sent to our servers

### Security Measures
- All data stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs
- No transmission over networks (except Chrome Sync, which is encrypted)
- No third-party analytics or tracking scripts
- Open-source code available for security audits

### Data Retention
- Data persists until you delete it or uninstall the extension
- Uninstalling removes all local data immediately
- Synced data is removed from Chrome Sync per Google's policies

## Your Rights

### Access and Control

You have the right to:

- ✅ **Access**: View all data stored by the extension (Settings pages)
- ✅ **Modify**: Change any settings or data at any time
- ✅ **Delete**: Remove specific data or all data by uninstalling
- ✅ **Export**: Manually export settings (via Chrome DevTools)
- ✅ **Opt-Out**: Disable features or the entire extension anytime

### How to Exercise Rights

1. **View Data**: Open Settings and review each tab
2. **Delete Specific Data**: Use the remove buttons in Settings
3. **Delete All Data**: Uninstall the extension from `chrome://extensions/`
4. **Disable Features**: Toggle off specific modes in Settings
